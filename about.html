About Velvet Charms

Velvet Charms is a small handmade brand focused on cozy, thoughtful creations. Each item is crafted with care, patience, and attention to detail — from candles and natural soaps to knitted pieces and decorative creations.

Many of our products are made in small batches, and we also welcome custom commissions. If you have a specific idea, size, scent, color palette, or gift concept in mind, feel free to reach out.

Orders & Payments

Orders are placed directly through our website. Payments are processed securely via PayPal, allowing you to pay using your PayPal balance or debit/credit card.

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive updates regarding preparation and delivery. Handmade items may require a short processing time — quality always comes first.

Contact

For commissions, questions, or special requests, please use the Contact page. We usually respond within 24–48 hours.
