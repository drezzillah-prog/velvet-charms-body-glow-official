About Velvet Charms

Velvet Charms is a small handmade studio creating cozy, thoughtful pieces with care, patience, and attention to detail.

Our collections include natural candles, artisanal soaps, knitted winter wear, body care items, epoxy and clay creations, as well as personalized commissions. Every item is made individually or in small batches — never mass-produced.

Custom orders & commissions

Many of our pieces can be customized, from subtle adjustments to fully bespoke creations. If you have something specific in mind, feel free to contact us and tell us your idea.

Ordering & payment

Items can be ordered directly from the catalogue and paid for immediately. If you request customizations, we first discuss the details together, after which you receive a personalized payment link.

Custom pieces are created only after payment is completed, as they are made specifically for you.
