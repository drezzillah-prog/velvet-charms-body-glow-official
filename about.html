About Velvet Charms

Velvet Charms is a handmade studio dedicated to creating cozy, meaningful pieces — candles, soaps, knitted wear, paintings, epoxy and mixed-media creations — crafted slowly and intentionally. Nothing here is mass-produced.

Custom creations are our specialty

You may purchase an item exactly as shown in the catalogue, or use it as inspiration for a personalized version made just for you.

How custom orders work

  1. Choose your inspiration
    Select an item from our catalogue or share your own ideas, photos, or sketches.
  2. Describe your vision
    Colors and aromas are complimentary. Changes in size, materials, or structure may affect pricing.
  3. Visual preview
    You receive concept visuals or a 3D-style preview before anything is made.
  4. Adjustments
    Refinements are welcome until everything feels just right.
  5. Creation begins
    Once approved, your piece is handcrafted exclusively for you.

Single items can be purchased instantly via secure checkout. Multiple items or custom requests are reviewed and combined into a bespoke order, paid through a personalized payment link that reflects the final agreed details.
