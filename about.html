About Velvet Charms

Velvet Charms is a small, slow-made studio focused on handcrafted pieces that feel warm, personal, and thoughtfully created.

From body care and cozy knits to art objects and decorative keepsakes, each item is made with patience, texture, and attention to detail.

Ordering & Payment

Some Velvet Charms items can be purchased exactly as shown, while others are customizable or created entirely from scratch.

Catalogue prices reflect the standard version.
Colors and aromas are always included at no extra cost. Size changes, added elements, or complex detailing may affect pricing.

For multiple items or custom requests, simply contact us. We will prepare a single personalized payment link reflecting the final agreed details.

Larger commissions may include a thank-you adjustment, applied directly to your personalized payment link.
