About Velvet Charms

Velvet Charms is a handmade studio dedicated to creating cozy, meaningful pieces — candles, soaps, knitted wear, paintings, epoxy and mixed-media creations — crafted slowly and intentionally, never mass-produced.

Custom creations are our specialty

Every item can be personalized. You may choose a piece exactly as shown in the catalogue, or use it as inspiration for something uniquely yours.

How custom orders work

  1. Choose your inspiration
    Select an item from our catalogue or upload your own photos, sketches, or ideas.
  2. Describe your vision
    Share colors, size, materials, mood, and any personal details you want included. Colors and aromas are always complimentary.
  3. Visual preview
    We’ll send concept images or a 3D-style visual of your custom piece.
  4. Adjustments
    You can request changes until it feels just right.
  5. Creation begins
    Once approved, we begin crafting your one-of-a-kind piece by hand.

Single items can be purchased instantly via secure checkout. Multiple items or larger commissions are carefully reviewed and combined into a bespoke order. The total amount is provided via a personalized payment link, including any applicable thank-you adjustment.
