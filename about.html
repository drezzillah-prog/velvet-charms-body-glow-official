About Velvet Charms

Velvet Charms is a small handmade studio dedicated to creating cozy, tactile pieces meant to be lived with, gifted, and cherished.

Every item is crafted slowly and thoughtfully — from sculptural candles poured in small batches, to hand-cut soaps scented with essential oils, knitted winterwear brushed for softness, and mixed-media artworks that blend paint, clay, and texture.

We focus on materials that feel gentle and intentional, favoring detail, warmth, and character over mass production. No two pieces are ever exactly alike — and that’s the point.

Custom creations are our specialty

Many of our pieces are made to order. Whether you have a color palette in mind, a personal motif, or a gift idea that needs a personal touch, we’re happy to create something just for you.

How custom orders work

  1. You contact us with your idea or inspiration.
  2. We discuss materials, sizing, colors, and timeline.
  3. You receive a visual preview or confirmation.
  4. Your piece is handcrafted and prepared with care.

Custom orders take a little extra time — but the result is something uniquely yours.
