Our approach

Everything you see is crafted with attention to detail and a love for texture: sculptural candles, hand-cut soaps, brushed wool knits, and carefully balanced body care formulas. Many items are made to order and can be personalized.

Ordering & payment information

Because most Velvet Charms items are handmade and customizable, each product is purchased individually. This ensures every detail is created exactly as requested.

Want to order multiple items or request customization? Simply contact us with the items you love and your preferences. We’ll create a single personalized payment link for you.

This process allows flexibility, accurate pricing, and a smooth ordering experience tailored to each customer.
